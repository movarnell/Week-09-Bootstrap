The Games We Play

We play on both PC and Nintendo Switch Systems.

Some even bring their own Xboxes, but we don't speak of that.

Mario Kart 8

A racing video game in which players can choose characters from the Mario franchise and race go-karts on different tracks while using various power-ups to gain an advantage.

Super Smash Bros.

A fighting game featuring popular characters from various video game franchises battling on various stages.

Madden NFL

A popular American football video game franchise that allows players to simulate the sport and control their favorite teams.

Rocket League

TA high-powered hybrid of arcade-style soccer and vehicular mayhem with easy-to-understand controls and fluid, physics-driven competition.

League of Legends

A multiplayer online battle arena game where players choose from a vast selection of champions with unique abilities to battle against a team of enemy players and their base.
