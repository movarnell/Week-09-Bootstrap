Why E-Sports?

E-sport teams in high school are competitive gaming teams made up of students who compete against other schools in various online video games. These teams are becoming increasingly popular as the world of gaming continues to grow and gain mainstream acceptance. In e-sport competitions, high school teams typically compete in popular games such as League of Legends, Overwatch, and Fortnite. E-sport teams offer students a chance to engage in a fun and challenging activity while also promoting teamwork, communication, and strategy. Additionally, participating in e-sports can also help students develop important skills like critical thinking, problem-solving, and decision-making.
